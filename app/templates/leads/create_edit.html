Lead Information
What is a Lead?

A lead represents a potential customer interested in purchasing a vehicle from the dealership.

  • Contact details are used for communication
  • Status tracks the progress of the lead through the sales funnel
  • Vehicle interests help understand what the customer is looking for
Tips for Lead Management
  • Response time is critical - contact new leads within 15 minutes
  • Record all interactions with the lead in the notes section
  • Update the lead status as you progress through the sales process
