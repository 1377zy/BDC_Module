About Follow-Up Sequences
What are Follow-Up Sequences?

Follow-up sequences are automated workflows that help you nurture leads through a series of predefined steps with specific timing.

How Do They Work?

Each sequence contains steps that can send emails, SMS messages, or create tasks for your team at specified intervals.

Benefits

Consistent follow-up, time savings, improved conversion rates, and better lead nurturing without manual effort.

Best Practices
  • Create different sequences for different lead sources or stages
  • Keep your sequences concise - 3-5 steps is usually optimal
  • Space out your communications appropriately (don't overwhelm leads)
  • Use a mix of communication channels (email, SMS, phone calls)
  • Regularly review sequence performance and make adjustments
