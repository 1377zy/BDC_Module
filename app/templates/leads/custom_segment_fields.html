About Custom Fields

Custom fields allow you to define additional attributes for leads that can be used for segmentation. For example, you might want to track a lead's preferred contact method, vehicle color preference, or any other information specific to your dealership.

Once you create a custom field, you can add values for individual leads from their edit page, and then use these fields to create segments.
