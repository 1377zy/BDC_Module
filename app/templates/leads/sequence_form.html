Sequence Tips
  • Use a clear, descriptive name for your sequence
  • Start with a welcome or introduction message
  • Space out steps appropriately (1-3 days apart)
  • Mix communication channels for better engagement
  • Include clear calls-to-action in each message
  • End with a compelling final offer or opportunity
